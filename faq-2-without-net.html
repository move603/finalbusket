Frequently Asked Questions

To download a movie, first search for it in our library. Click on the movie you want, then select the download option. Choose your preferred quality and format, then click the download button. The file will be saved to your device's downloads folder.

Yes! We offer streaming options for most movies in our library. Simply click the "Watch Online" button instead of download. You can stream in various qualities depending on your internet connection speed.

If a download link isn't working, try these troubleshooting steps:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
  • Disable any ad blockers temporarily
  • Use our mirror links if available
  • Contact support if the issue persists

We prioritize user safety and regularly scan all files for viruses and malware. However, as with any download from the internet, we recommend using antivirus software and exercising caution. We are not responsible for any issues that may arise from downloaded content.

Basic browsing and searching is available without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to:

  • Save your favorite movies
  • Track download history
  • Receive notifications about new releases
  • Access premium content (if available)

If you encounter a missing or broken movie, please:

  1. Use our "Report Issue" form to notify us
  2. Include the movie title and specific problem
  3. Check our "Movie Request" section to suggest it
  4. Our team reviews reports within 24-48 hours

To request a movie or TV show:

  1. Go to our Movie Request page
  2. Fill in the movie details (title, year, etc.)
  3. Provide a link to IMDb or TMDB for verification
  4. Submit your request
  5. You'll receive an email when it's added

We aim to fix reported issues within 24-48 hours during business days. Complex issues or content that needs to be re-uploaded may take longer. You'll receive an email notification once the issue is resolved.

Most movies in our library are available for free streaming and download. Some premium or recent releases may require a subscription or one-time payment. Pricing information is clearly displayed on each movie page.

Subtitles are available for most movies. Look for the "Subtitles" or "CC" button during playback or download. We support multiple languages. If subtitles are missing for a particular movie, you can request them through our support channels.

If you need to request removal of content (DMCA takedown):

  1. Go to our DMCA Request page
  2. Fill out the removal request form
  3. Provide all required information and evidence
  4. Submit the form
  5. Our legal team will review within 24-48 hours

Our platform supports:

  • Desktop computers (Windows, Mac, Linux)
  • Mobile devices (iOS, Android)
  • Tablets
  • Smart TVs (select models)
  • Streaming devices (Roku, Fire TV, etc.)

For the best experience, use a modern browser and ensure you have a stable internet connection.
